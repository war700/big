Budgetplanner

The budget planner helps you to create an overview of your income and expenses. Only fill in the boxes that are necessary; you can leave the others blank.

NOTE: This budget planner does not have a save or export function. If you want to save your data, you will need to write them down on a document. However, this website can always perform the necessary calculations for you, even if you later change, delete, or add certain amounts.

1. Income

What is your income? How much do you earn? Or what benefits do you receive?

2. Expenses

How much do you spend per month on your house, car, fixed costs, food, and drinks?

3. Savings

Try to save every month for something you would like to have or to cope with unexpected events in your life.

4. Result

How much money do you have left at the end of the month? Or do you not make ends meet at the end of the month?
